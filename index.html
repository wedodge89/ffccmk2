FINAL FANTASY CREATURE COLLECTOR

At the start of the game, you will be given a random Goal Number.

There are four Creature Buttons below. Each time you click a creature button, it will add a specific, randomized value to your Current Score.

You win when your Current Score matches the Goal Number.

Each creature's value is a mystery until you click on it, and it resets each time you restart the game.

Good luck!
